# Deep Navy App Icon Design

## Goal

Create a clean Android launcher icon for SmartGym that remains recognizable at small sizes.

## Composition

- Square 1:1 canvas designed for Android adaptive-icon cropping.
- One centered, symmetrical barbell with generous safe-area padding.
- Simple plate geometry and a strong horizontal silhouette.
- No letters, words, border frame, watermark, people, or extra gym equipment.

## Visual direction

- Background: smooth deep-navy gradient from `#050816` to `#14213D`.
- Barbell: cool electric blue to cyan highlights with a restrained neon edge.
- High contrast without aggressive bloom, lens flare, or tiny decorative detail.
- Premium, modern, minimal, and consistent with the app's dark-neon Home screen.

## Deliverable

- One polished square PNG concept at high resolution.
- The subject must remain legible when reduced to launcher-icon size.
- The generated concept is saved as a new project asset and does not overwrite the current launcher resources automatically.
